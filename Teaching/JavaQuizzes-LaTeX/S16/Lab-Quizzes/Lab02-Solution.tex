\item \pts{2} Convert 126_10. Show your work.
\newline
\Ans \newline
126  /  2 = 63,  Rem: 0 \newline
63  /  2 = 31,  Rem: 1 \newline
31 / 2 = 15,  Rem: 1 \newline
15 / 2 = 7, Rem: 1 \newline
7 / 2 = 3, Rem: 1 \newline
3 / 2 = 1, Rem: 1 \newline
1 / 2 = 0, Rem: 1 \newline
